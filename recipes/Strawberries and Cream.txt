Strawberries and Cream Oatmeal
This strawberries and cream oatmeal will keep you full until lunchtime or beyond. Packed with fiber and sweetened with brown sugar, this oatmeal makes your taste buds think they are eating dessert.

By Yolanda Gutierrez
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 5 mins
Total Time: 15 mins
Servings: 4
Ingredients
1 1/2 cups diced strawberries, divided

1 cup milk

1 cup water

2 tablespoons brown sugar, or to taste

1 pinch salt

1 cup oatmeal

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions
Place 3/4 cup strawberries, milk, water, brown sugar, and salt in a blender cup and blend until smooth.

Place oatmeal and strawberry mixture in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, add remaining strawberries, and simmer until thick, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in vanilla.
