Microwave Chocolate Pudding Cake
This microwave chocolate pudding cake is chocolatey, rich, and indulgent with both a pudding layer and a cake layer. Make sure you serve it with vanilla ice cream! It's the perfect pairing.

By Juliana Hale
 Tested by Sarah Brekke
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 20 mins
Servings: 6
Ingredients
Topping:

1 1/2 cups hot water

1 cup packed dark brown sugar

1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

1/4 cup salted butter, cubed

Cake:

1 cup all purpose flour

1 cup white sugar

1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder

1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

1/2 teaspoon instant espresso coffee powder

1 cup milk

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup vanilla ice cream, or to taste

Directions
Gather all ingredients. 
For topping, combine hot water, brown sugar, 1/4 cup cocoa powder, and butter in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high until bubbly, 4 minutes.

For cake, whisk together flour, white sugar, 1/3 cup cocoa powder, baking powder, salt, and instant espresso powder in a 2-quart round casserole dish. Add milk and vanilla; whisk until smooth. 

Pour topping over the cake batter, they will flow together. 
Microwave on high until the cake is on top of the sauce, 8 to 10 minutes.

Serve warm topped with vanilla ice cream.
