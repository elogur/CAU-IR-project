Caramel Apple Overnight Oats
This sweetly spiced breakfast will get you in the fall spirit.

By Bailey Fink
 Tested by Liz Mervosh
Prep Time: 15 mins
Chill Time: 8 hrs
Total Time: 8 hrs 15 mins
Servings: 4 (serving size: about 1 1/4 cup oatmeal mixture)
Ingredients
2 cups uncooked old-fashioned regular rolled oats

2 cups apple cider

1 cup plain whole-milk strained (Greek-style) yogurt

3 tablespoons caramel sauce, plus more for drizzling

1 tablespoon chia seeds

3/4 teaspoon apple pie spice

2 cups finely chopped unpeeled Granny Smith apples, divided

Mini candy-coated milk chocolate pieces (such as M&Mâ€™S), chopped nuts, shredded coconut (optional)

Directions
Gather all ingredients.

Stir together oats, apple cider, yogurt, caramel sauce, chia seeds, apple pie spice, and 1 cup of the apples in a medium bowl until combined.

Drizzle caramel sauce inside 4 (12-oz.) jars.

Spoon oat mixture evenly into prepared jars (about 1 1/4 cups each). Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 8 hours or up to 4 days.

When ready to serve, top each with 1/4 cup remaining apples and drizzle with additional caramel sauce. Top as desired.
