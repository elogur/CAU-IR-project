Cauliflower Popcorn
Cauliflower popcorn is an easy and good after-school snack. My kids love it!

Submitted by Meg Mitchell
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 25 mins
Servings: 4
Ingredients
1 tablespoon olive oil

Â½ teaspoon garlic salt

1 large head cauliflower, broken into small florets

Directions
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

Whisk olive oil and garlic salt together in a large bowl; add cauliflower and toss to coat completely. Spread cauliflower onto a baking sheet.

Bake in the preheated oven until golden and tender, 15 to 18 minutes.
