Mexican Oatmeal
This Mexican oatmeal brings back memories of when my mother made it for us when we were kids. Made with milk, cinnamon, and vanilla, itâ€™s sweet, creamy, and comforting.

By Yolanda Gutierrez
Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 15 mins
Servings: 1
Ingredients
1 cup water

1 Mexican cinnamon stick

1/2 cup quick-cooking oats

1/2 cup milk

1 tablespoon piloncillo, or to taste

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions
Bring water and cinnamon stick to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.

Add oats and cook for 1 minute. Add milk and cook until thickened, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir in piloncillo, cinnamon, and vanilla.
